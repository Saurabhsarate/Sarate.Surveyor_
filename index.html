WHAT WE DO

Our Expertise

Comprehensive surveying solutions for civil and structural engineering.

Gas Pipeline Survey

Specialized route planning, geotechnical analysis, and safety inspections for high-pressure gas pipelines (HPCL/BPCL).

Water Pipeline Survey

Precise alignment, elevation mapping, and hydraulic analysis for municipal and industrial water distribution networks.

Boundary Survey

Accurate demarcation of property lines to resolve disputes and facilitate real estate transactions and fencing.

Topographic Survey

Detailed contour mapping capturing natural and man-made features, essential for land development and architectural design.

Road Survey

L-Section and Cross-Section profiling for highway construction, pavement analysis, and traffic flow evaluation.

DGPS & Total Station

Using Differential GPS and robotic Total Stations for millimeter-level accuracy in large-scale infrastructure projects.
